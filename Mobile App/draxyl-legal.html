All data transmitted between your device and Draxyl servers is protected using:

  • TLS 1.3: Latest transport layer security protocol
  • Perfect Forward Secrecy: Unique session keys that can't be compromised retroactively
  • Certificate Pinning: Prevents man-in-the-middle attacks on mobile apps

2.2 Encryption at Rest

Data stored on our servers is encrypted using:

  • AES-256: Industry-standard symmetric encryption
  • Key Management: Hardware Security Modules (HSMs) for key protection
  • Database Encryption: Transparent data encryption for all databases

2.3 End-to-End Encryption (E2EE)

For sensitive communications, optional E2EE ensures only you and your recipients can read messages:

  • Messages encrypted on your device before transmission
  • Draxyl cannot access encrypted content
  • Available for 1:1 chats and small groups
